Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Sweet, gooey, and pure deliciousness. The chocolate chip cookie is a quintessential American dessert. The flavor profile consist of deep, toasted notes of brown sugar and vanilla. When baked by my girlfriend, they are perfect and make my stomach happy.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 3/4 cup (1.5 sticks) unsalted butter browned
  • 3/4 cup light or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps

Dough procedure
  1. In a bowl, whisk all dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, salt.
  2. In a smaller bowl, add brown sugar, granulated sugar, and browned butter. Whisk with a handheld mixer until smooth.
  3. Add in the egg and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture.
  4. Mix together the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients.
  5. Add chocolate chips
Baking procedure
  1. Leave the dough in the fridge for 1 hour to chill it.
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  4. Add dough balls to the sheets and press them down slightly. Bake them for 12-15 minutes.

Let the cookies rest after baking and enjoy!
